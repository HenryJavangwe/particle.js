Love for Sports Cars

Sports Cars | Super Cars | Muscle Cars

Among a lot of other things, one of my top favorite topics would be Cars. Very powerful and insanely cars.
My love for cars is not unjustified, who wouldn't want a v12 3/5-second super car? And if you can have the best of both worlds by coupling up that up with a v8 5-8 second Muscle, wouldn't that be the very essence of true love and true happiness? Just kidding lol.

Ever since I can remember, I have always loved being behind the steering wheel and often fantasized on the day I would get my first car and how I would set off on the longest road trip ever recorded.
Whether it's images, video reviews or magazine articles, I tend to get drawn in. I am a big super and muscle car fanatic. A genuine believer in speed and horsepower. If I had been born under different with access to vehicles, I could have chosen a career in F1 racing, maybe
